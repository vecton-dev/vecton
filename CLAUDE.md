This repository is for **Vecton**'s corporate site (コーポレートサイト). Vecton is a software development company that provides contract development (受託開発) and product development (プロダクト開発), positioning itself around structured thinking — clarifying requirements before writing code.

## LP Structure (from specs_lp.md)

The landing page follows this flow:
1. **ファーストビュー** — Hero with headline "方向を、設計する。" + CTA "相談する"
2. **課題提起** — Pain points (ambiguous requirements, biz/dev misalignment)
3. **提供価値** — Three-step value: 整理する → 設計する → 実装する
4. **サービス** — 受託開発 / プロダクト開発
5. **強み** — Three differentiators
6. **実績** — Case studies (placeholder, to be replaced)
7. **CTA** — Contact form / inquiry button

## Brand Voice

- Concise, structural, minimal Japanese
- Tagline direction: "方向を、設計する。" (primary candidate)
- Tone: confident, analytical, not salesy
